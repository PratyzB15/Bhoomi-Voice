# **App Name**: Bhoomi Voice

## Core Features:

- Linguistic Portal: Onboarding interface for language selection across five languages (English, Hindi, Bengali, Tamil, Marathi) that persists across all AI text and voice outputs.
- Bhoomi-STT/TTS Pipeline: Seamless bidirectional voice communication allows farmers to speak their queries and hear synthesized AI responses in their chosen regional language.
- Crop Vision Diagnostic: Image and file upload capability allowing the AI to analyze plant health and suggest specific organic remedies using computer vision.
- Predictive Guidance Tool: Reasoning tool that interprets user intent to suggest related topics like best seeds, current market rates, and irrigation timing based on their initial query.
- Localized Intelligence Engine: RAG-powered system that queries verified agricultural data for India-specific subsidies, weather-aligned planting calendars, and current crop market prices.
- Natural Farming Advisor: Educational resource explaining multi-level cropping strategies and seasonal irrigation amounts formatted in clean, easy-to-read bullet points.

## Style Guidelines:

- The palette is 'Earthy Vitality'—a light scheme featuring a Deep Foliage Primary (#2E5B3D) on a Soft Pearl background (#F4F7F2) with an Harvest Gold accent (#D4A017).
- A font pairing of 'Playfair' (Serif) for sophisticated headers to provide weight and importance, and 'PT Sans' (Sans-serif) for high-readability body text suitable for functional farm data.
- Rounded, soft-edged agricultural icons (leaves, water drops, market stalls) that provide clear visual cues for non-literate navigation assistance.
- A focused, center-aligned mobile interface 'Stage' layout that mimics a premium handheld application while masking the underlying complex data structures.
- Lush, gentle swaying plant animations in the background and soft transitions for chat bubbles to evoke a sense of organic growth and empathy.
